The American Red Cross

The American Red Cross is a national organization that gives natural disaster assistance, emergency aid, and works to educate people in various subjects across the US.It gives various oppurtunities to teens where they can help out or give service to administrative services, blood drives, fundraising events, disaster services, the armed forces and much more.

  • WHEN: Depends on program
  • WHERE: 520 W. 49th St. New York, NY
  • CONTACT: 877-REDCROSS

Mount Sinai Hospital

Mount Sinai Hospital offers volunteering for teens aged 14 and up. They cover various subject areas ranging from cancer centers to office centers to communicating with patients. The hospital also allows teen to have free parking, lunch, flu vaccination, and tuberculosis.

  • WHEN: FLEXIBLE BASED ON YOUR SCHEDULE
  • WHERE: E 101st Street, New York, NY 10029
  • CONTACT: (212) 241-6500
