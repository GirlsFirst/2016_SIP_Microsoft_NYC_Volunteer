Food Pantry Volunteer Opportunities

Citymeals On Wheels

Citymeals On Wheels is a nonprofit organization that provides the elderly in New York City with food. It has 31 meal centers across the city and last year they provided food to about 18, 000 senior citizens. Teens can volunteer at this organization by delivering meals or engaging in recreational activities with the elderly.

  • WHEN: Daily 9 a.m. to 12 p.m
  • WHERE: 355 Lexington Ave. New York, NY 10017
  • CONTACT: (212) 687-1234
For more information, check out City on Wheels's website: HERE!

Food Bank for New York City

The Food Bank for New York City is a nonprofit organization that works to provide the poor New York City residence with food and drinks. Over the years, it has received numerous awards and got national recognition when it was listed under "200 Largest U.S. Charities for 2011" in the Forbes magazine. Teens can easily volunteer in the organization's Warehouse Repack Program, the kitchen, and the food storage section.

  • WHEN: DEPENDING ON TYPE OF VOLUNTEERING JOB
  • WHERE: 39 Broadway New York, NY 10006
  • CONTACT: (212) 566-7855
For more information, check out Food Bank for New York City's website: HERE!

The Holy Apostles Soup Kitchen

The Holy Apostles Soup Kitchen provides food for the homeless in New York City. Teens can easily join the organization and help out in the process of distributing food, giving directions, keeping different records of certain factors, or just roam around to greet the homeless. It's possible to volunteer here as an individual or with a group.

  • WHEN: Monday to Friday 10:30am to 12:30pm
  • WHERE: 296 9th Ave, New York, NY 10001
  • CONTACT: (212) 924-0167
For more information, check out The Holy Apostles Soup Kitchen's website: HERE!

Meat Loaf Kitchen

The Meat Loaf Kitchen, launched in 1982, serves free food to New Yorkers, homeless or not. Volunteering here means, serving food, washing dishes,setting up tables, and so on.

  • WHEN: Saturdays Morning shift – 8 a.m. to Noon Afternoon shift – 11:50 a.m. to 3:30 p.m
  • WHERE: 137 E 2nd St, New York, NY 10009
  • CONTACT: (347) 850-2230
For more information, check out Meat Loaf Kitchen's website: HERE!

Educational Alliance

The Educational Alliance works to provide programs to people of all ages for them to grow in different subject area. Children, parents, and the elderly can take part in these programs for free. Volunteering here means the growth of social of communication skills.

  • WHEN: DEPENDS ON PROGRAM
  • WHERE: 197 E Broadway, New York, NY 10002
  • CONTACT: (212) 780-2300
For more information, check out the Educational Alliance's website: HERE!
